Taking the First Step

Reaching out for support can feel daunting. Whether you're dealing with anxiety, depression, relationship difficulties, or simply feeling stuck, therapy can help you gain clarity, develop coping strategies, and move forward with greater confidence.

How I Can Help

Anxiety & Stress

Learn to understand and manage anxiety, develop practical coping techniques, and build resilience for everyday challenges.

Depression

Work through feelings of low mood, hopelessness or loss of motivation in a supportive therapeutic relationship.

Relationship Issues

Explore patterns in your relationships, improve communication skills, and work towards healthier connections.

Life Transitions

Navigate major life changes such as career shifts, bereavement, separation, or becoming a parent.

Self-Esteem

Build confidence, challenge negative self-beliefs, and develop a kinder relationship with yourself.

Trauma

Process difficult past experiences in a safe environment at a pace that feels right for you.

About Claire

I'm a qualified psychotherapist with over 10 years experience working with adults experiencing a wide range of difficulties. My approach is integrative, drawing from different therapeutic models to tailor our work to your unique needs.

I believe that meaningful change happens within a trusting therapeutic relationship. My role is to provide a safe, confidential space where you can be truly heard without judgement.

Sessions are available in-person at my practice in Templeogue, or online via secure video call.

Qualifications & Accreditation

MA in Psychotherapy, Trinity College Dublin
Accredited Member, Irish Association for Counselling and Psychotherapy (IACP)
Registered with the Psychological Society of Ireland
