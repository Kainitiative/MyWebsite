Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take?

Typically 3–7 days once I have your content (text and photos).

Do I need to write the text?

It's best if you do, but bullet points are fine! I can turn them into proper website text for you.

Can I update it later?

Yes, just message or email me. Small updates are included in your monthly support.

Do you provide email addresses?

I can set up a professional email (e.g., info@yourbusiness.ie) as an optional add-on.

What about GDPR?

I include a standard cookie banner and privacy policy link to help you comply.

Do you provide domain names?

Yes. If you don't already have a domain, I'll register one for you as part of the setup — it's included in the €300 fee. The domain will be registered in your name.

What happens after the first year?

You'll receive a yearly invoice for your domain renewal (typically €15–€25 for .ie domains). I'll look after the technical side and make sure everything stays connected and working.
