1. Introduction

This privacy policy explains how Patrick Ryan Web Design ("I", "me", "my") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use my website design and hosting services.

I only collect personal information that is necessary to:

This typically includes your name, email address, phone number, business name, and billing address.

3. How I Use Your Information

Your personal information is used solely for:

Your personal information is stored securely and only for as long as necessary to provide my services. I take reasonable precautions to protect your data from unauthorised access, loss, or misuse.

This website may use cookies to remember your preferences (such as dark mode settings). No tracking or advertising cookies are used without your consent.
